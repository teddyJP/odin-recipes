Double Blueberry Muffins

Recipe Despcription

This recipe for baking double blueberry muffins was found from the Food Network website and is courtesy of Gale Gand. This recipe will take about an hour to bake and about another 15 minutes to prep. THe amount of muffins that will yield from this recipe is about 12.

Ingredients

  • 1 stick (8 tablespoons) soften unsalted butter
  • 1 cup and 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/2 cups fresh or thawed frozen blueberries
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Grease muffin tin
  3. With a mixer, cream the butter until it is smooth
  4. Add 1 cup of sugar and mix
  5. Add the eggs, vanilla, baking powder, and salt and mix together
  6. Mash 3/4 cup of blueberries with the back of a fork and add to the batter and mix
  7. Mix together 1/2 of the flower and then 1/2 of the milf and mix
  8. Repeat with remaining flour and milk
  9. Fold in remaining bleberries by hand until well mixed
  10. Mix 2 tablespoons of sugar and cinnamin in seperate bowl
  11. Using a large sppon, fill each muffin cup 3/4 full
  12. Sprinkle cinnamon-sugar combination over the muffins
  13. Bake until golden brown and risen ~25-30 minutes
  14. Let cool in pan for about 30 minutes before serving
